Choking

  • Check mouth for objects; remove if easy.
  • Do NOT push object further down.
  • Perform pet Heimlich if necessary.
  • Rush to vet immediately.

Poisoning

  • Identify the toxin if possible.
  • Do NOT induce vomiting unless told.
  • Keep packaging/sample of poison.
  • Call Vet or Poison Control.
